# theclovercafe_godot

DESCRIPTION
The Clover Cup is a café time management GODOT game with a creepy twist. Every night, creatures of all shapes and sizes come to you, desperate for their coffee fix. Your job is to serve as many monsters as possible before the 3 minutes are over.

HOW TO PLAY
1. Open full screen in Itch.io (https://rithunyaa.itch.io/the-clover-cafe)
2. Click the New Game button on the homescreen
3. Click the Next button after reading the instructions
4. Check their order bubble to see what they want and how many cups
5. Click the coffee machine to start brewing
6. Click the coffee machine again when it finishes to pick up the cup
7. Click on the customer to serve them

Earn coins when a full order is completed:
1 cup of coffee: 10 coins
2 cups of coffee: 30 coins

Watch the patience bar above each customer — if it runs out, they leave unhappy with no coins earned
Coins are only awarded when a full order is completed — serving one cup of a two-cup order earns nothing until the second cup is delivered.
